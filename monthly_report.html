SSDP Monthly Report - January 2026

Release Summary

2026-W02

➕ What we added:

  • UN156 Compliance & Automation: Introduced automatic processes for Service Activation and OTA in Garage to align with UN156 regulatory requirements. This includes the integration of RAIA API calls and support for informative file uploads during campaign drafts.

  • Cosmos DAC Full Suite: Deployed the complete P1/P2 integration for Cosmos DAC, covering LWM2M Gateway operations, Bootstrap sequences, and asynchronous Kafka notifications for vehicle registration and DM operations.

  • Enhanced Service Activation: Updated Service Activation and Quality Campaigns to handle SUPREM prescriptions including instances, providing a more granular and accurate service delivery model.

  • Time & Size Management: Implemented duration and package size calculations for campaigns, including default immobilization times and automated installation plan configurations.

  • OTA in Plant & Garage Expansion: * Plant: Added multi-VIN support and redundant UIN checking for TCU/HU units.

    • Garage: Exposed new APIs to abort campaigns directly from the garage and implemented workaround arbitration for ongoing campaigns.

🔧 What we changed:

  • Advanced Campaign Logic: * Refined the Chained Update flow with CSV upload capabilities and integrated time/size management into the multi-step wizard.

    • Campaigns now update VIN status based on precise Redbend (RB) codes and ERC lists.

  • UI/UX Optimization: * Reorganized the DOCG left panel by hiding legacy Factory Upgrade links in favor of the new FOTA Monitoring view.

    • Improved the "Vehicle ID Card" to include ECU replacement history and dynamic architecture naming.

  • RCZ & Drive Alerts: Migrated Drive Alert deletion APIs from DELETE to POST for partner/customer flows and implemented Kafka publishing for configuration IDs upon successful deletion.

🚮 What we Deprecated/ Removed:

  • Clone Functionality: Temporarily disabled the "Clone Campaign" action in certain DOCG modules to ensure data consistency during the migration to the new architecture.

  • Filtering Logic: Removed the filtering of identical object resource values across DM Campaign requests to ensure full transparency of requested changes.

🔨 What we fixed:

  • VSP & Campaign Execution: Resolved critical failures in VSP creation for IVI and multi-VIN factory upgrades. Fixed the issue where newly created OTA components were not registering on Redbend across DEV/INT environments.

  • Domain & Registration Stability: Fixed the "Change Domain" failure where moving a vehicle from Client to Preparation was not triggering correctly. Resolved several "NPE" (Null Pointer Exceptions) in ECU replacement flows.

  • Data Consistency: Patched the "Success/Fail" count mismatch on campaign cards and corrected the chronological ordering of campaigns in the Vehicle ID Card.

  • Connectivity & Validation: Ensured all connectivity options are available in Draft forms for Factory Upgrades and fixed the "Dummy Baseline" issue that affected campaign statuses.

 

2026-W04

➕ What we added:

  • Campaign Resilience: Implemented an automated Redbend Retry mechanism for OTA Campaigns to improve success rates during temporary synchronization failures between DOCG and Redbend.

  • VDP Optimization: Refactored the VDP_STORE (VDS1) logic to disable the Redis cache for state management in Staging, ensuring data consistency and readiness for the latest Patric integration.

 

🔧 What we changed:

  • Input Validation: Enforced a "No Special Characters" policy for Campaign Names in Production to prevent downstream processing errors and ensure compatibility with legacy internal systems.

  • Component Configuration: Updated the Backoffice to allow the addition of the specific SCOMO ID (R43000001) within OTA components, unblocking critical software configuration tasks.

🚮 What we Deprecated/ Removed:

 No explicit deprecations or removals were executed in this release.

🔨 What we fixed:

  • Service Activation Feedback: Resolved a production issue where Service Activation was failing to send updated status feedback to the Account Management (AM) module, ensuring proper end-to-end orchestration.

  • Telemetry & Location Accuracy: * Fixed a critical bug in VDP_STORE where location/position data was missing from the /telemetries endpoint.

    • Resolved an error in the APIM Trips API occurring when filtering telemetry data by specific value types.

  • Campaign & Tooling Accessibility: * Restored the "Check Inventory" button and options within the DOCG interface, which were previously unavailable to users.

    • Fixed a specific "Creation Failed" state for Quality Campaigns affecting high-priority VINs (NEAVV...).
